Model for a skid steer drive (wheels parallel with robot's direction of motion). When all motors are powered +100%, the robot should move forward in a straight line.

This constructor infers the two sensors from the left and right motors (using the integrated encoders).

Parameters
ileftSideMotorleft side motor
irightSideMotorright side motor
